  $(window).scrollTop()
        The portion of the document that has scrolled off-screen above the currently-visible
        portion.
        $(window).height()
        The height of the visible area of the document on-screen.
        $(document.body).height()
        The height of the body tag (i.e., the height of the entire page, on- and off-screen).
        $(window).scrollTop() + $(window).height() >= $(document).height()
        Will be true whenever you have scrolled to where the document is smaller than the
        visible window.
